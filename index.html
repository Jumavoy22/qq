Agrobank Business Copilot

Business client AI assistant that turns chaotic conversations into compliant banking documents via messengers

1 · Problem

The Digital Divide Blocks Growth

  • Skill gap: Complex CRMs & cloud tools intimidate relationship-driven managers.
  • Voice culture: Critical decisions are trapped in 30-second voice notes, not systems.
  • Compliance risk: Documentation lags behind conversations; banks lose visibility.
  • Remote friction: Clients want instant answers online, not long queues at branches.

2 · Solution

Bring the Bank Into Their Chats

  • Auto-workflows Create folder in the cloud, create CRM card, generate contract, send to client via messenger apps
  • Multi-system Data Merging Connects Google Sheets, OneDrive documents, and CRM deals
  • CRM Integration via Cloud Services Links files, auto-creates folders
  • Voice-to-Document AI Uzbek and Russian speech recognition, LLM intent extraction, document generation
